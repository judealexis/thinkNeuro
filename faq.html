FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The main perks of participation in Think Neuro’s programs include:
 1. Authorship on scholarly publications from research-based internships
 2. Academic scholarships from Medical Hackathon participation
For more information, please refer to the “Programs” section of our website.

Internship applications are open and accessible twice a year in the Fall and Spring. Clinical research applications are open and accessible twice a year in the Winter and Summer. Medical Hackathon applications are live in November.

Ambitious high school students and undergraduates are eligible to apply for the internship.

All applications are reviewed periodically. We typically have two review rounds and encourage early submissions. To gauge competitiveness of previous cohorts, please review our Fall 2023 Internship Admission Statistics.

You can find past oral and posters presented at the Think Neuro Research Symposium under the “Programs” section of our website. For research and scholarship testimonials, please follow our social media (thinkneuro.usa).
