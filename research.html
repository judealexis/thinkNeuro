ABOUT
Think Neuro offers clinical research projects pertaining to Bibliometrics, the study of productivity within a certain subfield of medicine. By enrolling in our Clinical Research Internship, you will learn how to code and analyze data using the R programming language along with gaining proficiency in operating Web of Science Core Collection (WoSCC). All researchers have their names listed on a research poster at our Symposium as well as deliver an oral presentation of their work. If you are a high school or undergraduate student interested in our Winter Clinical Research Internship, please watch our free seminar here to gauge if this program is a good fit for you and your career aspirations. If so, please reference this guide created by our staff to fill out the application below.
