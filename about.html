Michael Yan

Chief Executive Officer

Michael is a second-year undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley studying Molecular and Cellular Biology with a minor in Public Policy. With professional interests in politics and health economics, he aims to pursue a career in health policy and clinical research as a physician-economist. As the CEO of Think Neuro, he aims to democratize health information at the grassroots level, drive structural change within healthcare and Medicare policy, and forge intergenerational communities by connecting with populations often overlooked in the education space, particularly children of uninsured, low-income, and limited-English proficiency (LEP) households. Outside of academics, Michael enjoys golfing on weekday mornings and sing-songwriting as a true tenor.
